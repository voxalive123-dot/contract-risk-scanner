# Phase 11 Final Closure Checkpoint

## Closure Position

VoxaRisk is closed at this checkpoint as a governed MVP-plus contract risk intelligence system. The system combines deterministic contract-risk scanning with account, billing, entitlement, team, diagnostics, and internal operations controls. AI remains an explanatory layer only and does not alter deterministic scoring, severity, findings, or decision posture.

## Core System Included

- Deterministic contract-risk analyzer with evidence-backed findings, severity, flags, top risks, and scoring metadata.
- Expanded commercial rule coverage for material first-pass review signals.
- Public account identity bridge, controlled provisioning, password setup/reset, and authenticated account context.
- Organisation-level membership and team invite foundation with bounded owner/admin/member roles.
- Subscription truth, Stripe webhook reconciliation, billing customer references, billing portal linkage, and resolver-backed entitlement enforcement.
- Plan/quota enforcement before analysis work is consumed.
- AI Review Notes gated by paid entitlement and constrained to deterministic findings and evidence.
- Internal diagnostics, backfill tooling doctrine, operations runbook, internal operations console, and audited support workflow layer.

## Final Rule Expansion Families

Phase 11 Step 4 added or strengthened deterministic coverage for:

- Change-of-control assignment and counterparty transfer risk.
- Unilateral amendment and policy incorporation by reference.
- Fee acceleration and late-fee exposure.
- Service-credit sole-remedy traps.
- Publicity, logo, and customer-name use rights.
- Intrusive audit rights.
- Data retention and deletion asymmetry.
- Termination assistance and exit dependency.
- Survival clause risk concentration.

Each family is covered by positive, negative/carve-out, and edge/mixed phrasing tests.

## Production Seal Checks

The closure validation must include:

- Focused deterministic rule tests.
- API contract and deterministic scoring tests.
- Account, billing, Stripe, quota, AI, team, internal operations, workflow, diagnostics, and migration regression tests.
- Full backend test suite.
- Frontend lint and build when frontend files are present in the working checkpoint.

## Explicit Out Of Scope After Closure

- Lawyer-level semantic completeness.
- Legal advice, legal opinions, contract approval, certification, or safety guarantees.
- AI changing score, severity, findings, or deterministic decision logic.
- Broad CRM/support desk tooling.
- Broad admin mutation powers.
- Open public organisation signup.
- Advanced RBAC, SSO, self-serve team administration, or enterprise identity federation.
- Live legal-review workflow automation beyond governed risk intelligence.

## Known Limitations

- Regex/rule-based detection improves repeatability but cannot catch every drafting variation or commercial nuance.
- Negative-pattern suppression is deliberately conservative and may miss complex clause interactions.
- Uploaded or extracted text quality still affects scan quality.
- Billing/account operations remain governed but operationally narrow.
- Internal workflow actions are limited to notes and pending-invite cancellation.
- AI explanations depend on provider availability and remain subordinate to deterministic analysis.

## Recommended Post-Project Roadmap

- Add measured rule tuning from real anonymised customer false-positive/false-negative review.
- Add fuller customer billing/account self-service only after production usage proves the necessary surfaces.
- Add advanced team administration and roles only after the current membership model is exercised safely.
- Add operational monitoring dashboards from existing signals rather than broad CRM functionality.
- Add controlled AI readability/reporting improvements without changing deterministic authority.
