MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for an initial twelve-month term. Any renewal must be agreed in writing by both parties before the end of the then-current term.

Either party may terminate this Agreement for convenience on thirty (30) days' written notice. Either party may terminate immediately for material breach if the other party fails to cure that breach within thirty (30) days after written notice.

Fees are set out in the applicable Order Form and invoices are payable within thirty (30) days of receipt. No fee increase applies unless agreed in writing by both parties.

Each party's aggregate liability under this Agreement is limited to the fees paid in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim, except for fraud, wilful misconduct, confidentiality breach, and liabilities which cannot lawfully be limited.

Provider will use Customer Data only to provide the services and will return or delete Customer Data within thirty (30) days after termination, except to the extent retention is required by law.

Neither party may assign this Agreement without prior written consent of the other party, such consent not to be unreasonably withheld.
