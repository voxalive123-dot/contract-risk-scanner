SUBSCRIPTION SERVICES AGREEMENT

Provider grants Customer access to the hosted software platform, dashboard, API, and user accounts during the term.

The Agreement does not automatically renew. Any renewal must be agreed in writing by both parties before the end of the then-current term.

Fees are stated in the Order Form and are payable within thirty (30) days of receipt. Invoices are deemed accepted unless disputed within seven (7) days, and overdue balances may include an administrative charge. Undisputed amounts remain payable when due.

Provider commits to 99.9% uptime with service credits in addition to other remedies for repeated service level failures.

Provider may transfer Customer Data to subprocessors without prior notice where needed to operate the hosted service, and may use anonymised analytics for product development. Provider will return or delete Customer Data on request except as required by law.

Confidentiality obligations survive termination for twelve (12) months only.

Provider's aggregate liability is limited to fees paid in the prior twelve (12) months.

Manual validation note: this sample is intended to show medium commercial exposure from invoice mechanics, data-transfer/anonymisation rights, and confidentiality-survival imbalance without renewal lock-in, exit fees, IP ownership conflict, force majeure, liquidated damages, or restraint stacking.
